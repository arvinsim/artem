Vector Art 1: Kirby

vectorkirbyinkscape

This is an attempt to draw Kirby, a famous fictional character from Nintendo. I chose this character because it is easy to draw. But I underestimated how much time I needed to actually emulate the image. The gradients took most of my time since I am still not familiar with the software.

I used Inkscape to draw and Gpick to copy over the colors from the source image
